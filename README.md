# MoveStreak 
### *Consistency over Intensity. Every single day.*

MoveStreak is a premium, privacy-focused iOS application designed to transform sedentary routines into active lifestyles. Built with **SwiftUI** and powered by native Apple technologies, it bridges the gap between productivity and wellness through a sophisticated gamification engine and an adaptive difficulty system.

---

## The Philosophy: "Small Steps, Big Impact"
The modern workspace—whether at a desk or in a classroom—often encourages a sedentary lifestyle that impacts long-term health and focus. MoveStreak was engineered to fight this trend not by demanding hours at the gym, but by rewarding the **power of consistency**. 

By focusing on daily "streaks" and bite-sized guided activities, MoveStreak helps users build sustainable habits that stick, supported by a multi-sensory feedback system that makes progress feel tangible.

---

## Core Features

### 🚀 Adaptive Difficulty Engine
Unlike static fitness apps, MoveStreak evolves with you. The app features a native tracking system that monitors your progression across three distinct tiers:
- **Beginner (Levels 1–3)**: Focuses on establishing the habit.
- **Intermediate (Levels 4–7)**: Introduces longer activities and higher XP requirements.
- **Advanced (Levels 8+)**: Designed for users who have successfully integrated movement into their daily workflow.
*Difficulty scales automatically—no manual adjustments required.*

### Privacy as a Core Foundation
MoveStreak operates on a **Zero-Data-Collection** policy:
- **100% On-Device**: All movement statistics, hydration logs, and progress are stored exclusively on your iPhone.
- **No Cloud, No Compromise**: Your data never leaves your device and is never shared with third parties.
- **Zero Account Overhead**: No sign-ups, no tracking pixels, just movement.

### Holistic Wellness Tracking
- **Smart Hydration**: A dedicated module to ensure optimal cognitive function and physical health through water intake tracking.
- **Daily Guidance Engine**: A curated library of wellness tips covering posture, sleep hygiene, and stress management.
- **Grace Day System**: Life happens. MoveStreak includes an intelligent "Grace Day" mechanic to protect your hard-earned streak during unavoidable breaks.

### Gamification & Rewards
- **XP Ecosystem**: Earn Experience Points for every action—from walking to drinking water.
- **Milestone Achievements**: Unlock 25+ unique badges that celebrate specific milestones (e.g., "Weekend Warrior," "Hydration Hero," "Perfect Week").
- **Visual Progress**: Dynamic level-up celebrations powered by custom confetti engines and multi-sensory feedback.

## Technical Architecture

MoveStreak is a showcase of modern iOS development capabilities, utilizing:

| Technology | Implementation |
| :--- | :--- |
| **SwiftUI** | For a declarative, fluid, and fully responsive user interface. |
| **Core Motion** | Direct integration with device sensors for real-time step counting and activity detection. |
| **Swift Charts** | High-performance, native data visualizations for weekly activity breakdown. |
| **App Intents** | Native support for Siri Shortcuts and the Action Button, allowing users to log activities hands-free. |
| **AVFoundation** | Orchestrating a premium audio-visual feedback loop for timed activities. |
| **Haptic Engine** | Custom `UIImpactFeedback` patterns to provide tactile confirmation of actions. |

## Design & UX

The app follows a **Semantic Design System** that prioritizes clarity and accessibility:
- **SF Symbols Integration**: Using Apple’s native iconography for a consistent and familiar experience.
- **Accessibility (A11y)**: Fully localized strings, high-contrast support, and ScreenReader-ready components.
- **Micro-interactions**: Subtle animations and transitions that reduce cognitive load while enhancing user delight.

## Installation

MoveStreak is distributed as a **Swift Playgrounds Project (`.swiftpm`)**, making it exceptionally easy to inspect and run for developers and enthusiasts alike.

1.  **Clone** this repository to your local machine.
2.  Open the `MoveStreak.swiftpm` package using **Swift Playgrounds** (iPad/Mac) or **Xcode**.
3.  Ensure your target is an **iOS 17.0+** device or simulator.
4.  Build and Run.
